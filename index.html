Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your jersey, you can return it within 30 days of purchase.

  • Items must be unworn and in original condition
  • All original tags must be attached
  • Original packaging required
  • Return shipping fees apply (₹40)

Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes do you offer?

We offer sizes ranging from Small to XXL for most of our jerseys. Please check individual product pages for specific sizing availability.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unworn items with original tags. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

Are your jerseys authentic?

Yes, all our jerseys are authentic licensed products featuring official team logos and designs. We only source from authorized manufacturers.

Do you offer customization?

Yes, we offer name and number customization for most jerseys. Contact us for details and pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay for your convenience.
